What The Site Publishes

The deployed site is built from generated CSV reports, PNG plots, and a static dashboard. The methodology page documents the formulas behind the generated risk, valuation, curve, and execution measures.

  • Instrument PV, DV01, convexity, and key-rate DV01.
  • P&L explain from curve moves and full revaluation.
  • Intraday market replay and execution quality metrics.
  • Static pages deployable through GitHub Pages.
Pricing Bond cash flows, zero-rate discounting, yield-to-maturity solving, and vanilla swap valuation.
Risk DV01, pillar key-rate DV01, convexity, and curve shape measures for level, slope, and curvature.
Execution Order-book replay, arrival price, VWAP, TWAP, fill ratio, slippage, and shortfall.

All market data in the demo is synthetic or locally provided for methodology illustration.
