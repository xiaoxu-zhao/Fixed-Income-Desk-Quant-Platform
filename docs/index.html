What the simulator covers

A compact but technical rates workflow: deterministic inputs, explicit analytics, reproducible reports, and static visual outputs.

Full revaluation compared with first-order rates attribution and residual diagnostics.

Execution Analytics

Arrival, VWAP, TWAP, slippage, fill ratio, and shortfall from a deterministic hedge order.
